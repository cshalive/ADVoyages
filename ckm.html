The Land of Coffee

Chikmagalur is a serene hill station in Karnataka, known for its aromatic coffee plantations and breathtaking landscapes. Explore the sacred Baba Budangiri range, hike up to Mullayanagiri Peak, and witness the majestic Hebbe Falls. The Bhadra Wildlife Sanctuary is a haven for wildlife enthusiasts, offering a glimpse into the habitat of majestic elephants, elusive tigers, and graceful leopards.
